### Per-Call Capture Fields (all null until call occurs)

For each call, record:
- **Workflow:** What job are they trying to do?
- **Recurring pain:** What goes wrong repeatedly?
- **Current substitute:** What do they use today?
- **Frequency/cost:** How often and how much?
- **Buyer/champion:** Who would buy or champion this?
- **Switching barrier:** What prevents adoption?
- **Proof requested:** What evidence would they need to try it?
- **Objection:** What pushback do they raise?
- **WTP/procurement signal:** Any willingness-to-pay or budget signal?
- **Next action:** What follow-up was agreed?
- **Consent status:** Did they consent to being quoted?
- **Redacted evidence note:** Anonymized note for evidence corpus
