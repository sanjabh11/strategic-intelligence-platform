**Updated finding**: The whitespace has **narrowed but persists**. Feynn.ai now covers game theory + evidence + decision engine, which is the closest any competitor comes to SIP's full stack. However, the critical gaps remain:

### Updated Market Structure

The market is fragmenting into **5 distinct categories**, none of which fully overlap:

1. **Strategic Intelligence + Game Theory** (Feynn, SPIDR, SIP) — AI + game theory for decision-making
2. **Decision Governance** (DecisionLedger, Qonera, KiteEdge, Coalex, MagnaRix, Synalogic) — audit trails, human review, EU AI Act compliance
3. **Enterprise Forecasting** (Metaculus, Good Judgment, Cultivate Labs, Shreeng, FinHelm, Iridae) — Brier-scored prediction, calibration
4. **Geopolitical Intelligence** (Recorded Future, Palantir, World Monitor, CORVUS, Intelrift, GDELT Cloud, GeoQuant) — event data, risk monitoring
5. **Strategy Frameworks** (Jeda.ai, Entrapeer) — structured analysis, 300+ frameworks

**SIP's unique position**: Straddles categories 1, 2, and 3 — the only platform with game-theory analysis (cat 1) + human review governance (cat 2) + forecast governance with Brier scoring (cat 3). This is the **defensible niche**.
