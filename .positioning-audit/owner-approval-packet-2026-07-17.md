## 8. Education Privacy and Research/QI Classification

### Required Institutional Determination
- The activity's purpose, intended use, participant population, and data collection determine whether it is quality improvement, research, or another regulated activity.
- The relevant institution or its authorized IRB/human-research office—not this project or its agents—must make that determination.
- No student data may be collected before the institutional determination and explicit owner approval are recorded.
- Reference: [HHS OHRP Quality Improvement Activities guidance](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/education-and-outreach/faq-and-other-guidance/index.html).

### Student Privacy and Purpose Limitation
- Collect no student PII during discovery.
- Before any LMS or student-data access, require institutional privacy approval and document the authorized purpose.
- Keep the institution in control of any permitted education-record data.
- Prohibit reuse, sale, advertising use, or redisclosure outside the authorized purpose.
- Record the retention period and deletion method before collection.
- Separate QI activity from research in all case notes and retain the institutional determination.
- Reference: [U.S. Department of Education online educational services privacy guidance](https://studentprivacy.ed.gov/resources/protecting-student-privacy-while-using-online-educational-services-requirements-and-best).

### MobLab Complement (Not Replacement)
- Education positioning must remain complementary to [MobLab Classroom](https://www.moblab.com/products/classroom)
- MobLab provides: interactive games, assignments, gradebook, LMS integration
- This workflow provides: evidence-to-reasoning governance, actor reasoning, forecast-draft review
- No claims of replacing MobLab's classroom capabilities
