# Commercial Launch Readiness Audit - 2026-06-06

## Decision

Current decision: `pilot-only`.

This app is not yet `commercial-ready` because hosted/live proof is not present in this artifact, broad legacy RLS policies still need production-sensitive review, and prediction-accuracy claims are not backed by a resolved-outcome calibration ledger. It is credible for guided pilot outreach if the story stays narrow:

> Evidence-backed strategic intelligence for enterprise and public-sector decision teams: clarify the question, ground it in sources, build a strategist brief and countermove map, route contested/high-stakes output to human review, and carry the result into a governed forecast draft.
