# Market Differentiation Validation - 2026-06-06

## Decision

Current commercial posture: `pilot-only`.

The strongest worldwide marketable wedge is not "world-class accurate predictions" yet. The defensible wedge is:

> Governed strategic-intelligence workflow for enterprise and public-sector teams: clarify a high-stakes question, ground it in evidence, reason through actors and countermoves, route contested output through human review, and convert the result into a scoreable forecast draft.

This is stronger than a generic chatbot, a plain geopolitical dashboard, or an isolated forecasting board. It is weaker than enterprise incumbents on hosted security proof, integrations, real buyer validation, and resolved-outcome benchmark evidence.

## Marketable Strengths To Lead With

1. Evidence-backed strategic answer release rather than generic AI output.
2. Adaptive question sharpening before analysis, reducing shallow or mismatched predictions.
3. Actor, countermove, uncertainty, and forecast handoff in one workflow.
4. Human review and publication governance for high-stakes output.
5. Calibration, Brier, ledger, and benchmark mechanics already implemented as evidence paths.
6. Explicit degraded states and proof buckets, which are commercially valuable honesty signals.
7. Lightweight pilot shape compared with enterprise ontology or consulting-heavy alternatives.

## Claims To Avoid

- "World-class accurate predictions."
- "Enterprise-ready secure deployment."
- "Replacement for geopolitical intelligence teams."
- "Palantir-class operational AI."
- "ForecastBench/Metaculus/Good Judgment parity."
- "Live market-data intelligence" unless feed proof and freshness checks are attached.
