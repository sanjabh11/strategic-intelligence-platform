## What It Is

A governed strategic-intelligence pilot platform that connects evidence-gathering, game-theory analysis, human review, and forecast governance into one workflow. Built for enterprise strategy, geopolitical risk, and public-sector foresight teams who need defensible, source-backed decision support — not black-box AI predictions.
