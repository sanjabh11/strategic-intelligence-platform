## Pilot Agreement

This Pilot Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Ignite IT Serve** ("Provider") and **[Client Organization]** ("Client") for the purpose of testing the Strategic Intelligence Platform ("Platform") in a guided pilot program.

### 1. Pilot Scope

- **Objective**: Test the Platform's strategic-intelligence workflow on one high-stakes strategic question relevant to the Client's operations.
- **Use Cases**: 3 to 5 representative cases selected by mutual agreement.
- **Deliverables**: Evidence bundle, actor/countermove map, reviewer notes, and pre-resolution forecast register for each case.

### 2. Pilot Duration

- **Start Date**: [To be determined]
- **End Date**: [Start Date + 30 days]
- **Extension**: May be extended by mutual written agreement.

### 3. Success Criteria

The pilot will be evaluated against the following metrics:
- Time-to-brief: Reduction in time from question to decision-ready brief
- Decision usefulness: Qualitative assessment by Client's team
- Objection quality: Quality of objections and questions raised during review
- Pre-resolution forecast capture: Number of forecasts captured before outcome resolution

### 4. Data Handling

- **Client Data**: All data provided by Client remains owned by Client.
- **Analysis Outputs**: Analysis outputs (evidence bundles, briefs, forecasts) are owned by Client.
- **Platform Data**: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be used by Provider for product improvement.
- **Data Export**: Client may export all data at any time during the pilot.
- **Data Deletion**: Upon pilot termination, Client data will be deleted within 30 days unless Client requests earlier deletion.

### 5. Intellectual Property

- **Platform IP**: The Platform, including all software, algorithms, and documentation, remains the intellectual property of Provider.
- **Client IP**: Any Client-specific data, configurations, or custom scenarios remain the intellectual property of Client.
- **Pilot Insights**: Non-attributed insights about workflow patterns may be used by Provider for product improvement.

### 6. Pilot-Only Claim Boundary

Both parties acknowledge that:
- The Platform is in a pilot phase and is not represented as enterprise-ready, commercially-ready, or having world-class prediction accuracy.
- No buyer-validated willingness-to-pay claims are being made.
- The pilot is designed to test workflow utility, not to prove prediction accuracy or enterprise security.
- Results from the pilot should not be used to make public claims about the Platform's capabilities without mutual agreement.

### 7. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice.
- Upon termination, Client data will be deleted within 30 days.
- No fees are owed by either party upon termination during the pilot phase.

### 8. No Payment

- This pilot is provided at no cost to the Client.
- No payment is required during the pilot phase.
- Transition to paid services, if any, will be by separate agreement.

### 9. Confidentiality

- Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information shared during the pilot.
- Provider will not publicly name Client as a customer without Client's written consent.
- Client will not publicly disclose pilot results without Provider's written consent.

### 10. Limitation of Liability

- The Platform is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind.
- Provider's liability is limited to the fees paid during the pilot (zero).
- Client acknowledges that AI-generated analysis should be verified before making decisions.
